## ⚙️ Configuration

Open the settings menu to connect your audio sources. This tool supports various ways to bring your music into the game.

### Local Files
Select the folder containing your music files. The application scans these files and adds them to your playback queue. It supports standard formats such as MP3, WAV, and FLAC.

### Spotify
Log in with your your account details. This allows the application to pull your saved playlists and tracks directly into the interface. A browser window opens to confirm your permission for this connection.

### YouTube
Enter the link to your public playlists or set up your API key. The application streams the audio from these videos into your game session.

### Jellyfin
Provide your server address and login credentials. Once connected, your library appears in the app for immediate playback.

## 🎮 Using the Radio

Once you have your sources set up, open your game. Use the provided hotkeys to manage your audio while you drive. 

*   **Switch Tracks:** Press the forward or back keys to change the song.
*   **Volume Control:** Adjust the playback volume separate from the game volume.
*   **Source Toggle:** Swap between local files, Spotify, or your other connected services without pausing your game.

## 💡 Troubleshooting

Common issues often have simple fixes. Check this list if you experience trouble.

### Application Will Not Launch
Ensure you have the latest version of the software. Restart your computer to clear any locked processes that might prevent the app from starting.

### No Sound
Check your Windows sound mixer. Ensure the output is set to your preferred device. Verify that the application is not muted in the settings menu.

### Sync Issues
If your music stutters, check your internet connection. Large streaming libraries require consistent bandwidth. Lowering the audio quality in the settings can help if your connection feels unstable.

### Permission Errors
Run the application as an administrator if it cannot read your local music files. Right-click the shortcut and select Run as Administrator.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

This application interacts only with the services you authorize. It does not store your passwords on external servers. All authentication tokens stay on your local machine. The tool reads your local files to playback music and does not modify or delete your original media.
